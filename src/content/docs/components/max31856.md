---
title: MAX31856
description: Thermocouple-to-digital converter used on the thermocouple boards.
---

The reason we went with this over a more complex chip with more outputs (AD offers a few) is cost. The reason why we didn't go with raw amps was because of integration times, and because rolling our own would cost more in the long-run anyway. That being said, this chip has several limitations:

- Due to being unisolated, we have to add galvanic isolation to a whole module to prevent ground looping
- The sample rate is quite limited at ~10 Hz, which we should theoretically be able to push further especially with grounded thermocouples
