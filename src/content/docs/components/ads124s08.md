---
title: ADS124S08
description: 24-bit delta-sigma ADC used on the load-cell boards.
---

This is the ADC used on the load cells. It uses a delta-sigma 24-bit muxed design which supports up to 6 differenial inputs. We chose it because while it lacks AC excitation (where the polarity of a differential measurement is switched between each sample as to cancel out parasitics) the higher available PGA gain enables for much finer resolution. It also supports ratiometric measurements on the 5V reference rail which is critical for our use case.
