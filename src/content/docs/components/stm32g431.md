---
title: STM32G431
description: Cheap, modern CAN-FD microcontroller used for the flight (vehicle) hardware.
---

The G431 is just a nice cheap microcontroller which is somewhat modern. It's the favorite of several members in the club and people adjacent to the club, which is why we chose it to power our actual flight hardware. It also supports CAN-FD, which is critical for our use case.
