---
title: TCAN1462V
description: 3.3 V I/O CAN-FD transceiver used across the boards.
---

We have a lot of these already, and for the most part, this CAN-FD transceiver appears to be the same as a TCAN1472-Q1 but based on the older withdrawn CiA specification for SiC (signal-improvement-capable) instead opting for compliance with the newer IEC one. It supports +3.3V I/O for interfacing with our electronics, has a standby for when we don't use this on the ground station, and still uses normal +5V for communication.
