---
title: ADS131A04
description: Simultaneously sampling ~20 ENOB ADC used on the pressure-transducer boards.
---

This is the ADC used on the pressure transducers. It is a simultaneously sampling ~20 ENOB ADC which works great for our use case. It is fairly overkill and does not feature an internal PGA, although we only need unity gain for our PTs.
